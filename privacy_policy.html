Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 24, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the MotoBaana mobile application created by Heikki Turja (the "Service Provider") as a Free service. This service is intended for use "AS IS".

1. Information Collection and Use

1.1 Location Data

MotoBaana requires access to your device's location to provide core mapping features, including:

  • Displaying your current position on the map.

Data Handling: This location data is processed locally on your device. It is not transmitted to the Service Provider's servers or any third-party databases, unless you explicitly choose to publish a Community Route.

1.2 Technical Data

We may collect basic technical information to improve the Application, such as your device model and OS version. This data is handled ephemerally via our hosting providers.

2. Third-Party Services

The Application utilizes third-party services that have their own Privacy Policies:

  • Google Play Services
  • Cloudflare (Content Delivery)
  • Fintraffic / Finnish Transport Infrastructure Agency (Digitraffic): Provides real-time "Road Condition" (WMS) layers. To fetch these layers, the Application sends the geographic coordinates of the map area currently being viewed. This request also includes your IP address, which is used by their servers to deliver the data.
  • OpenStreetMap / MapLibre (Map Rendering)
  • Supabase (Database & Anonymous Auth for Community features)

3. Children's Privacy

This Application does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If we discover such data has been provided, we will delete it immediately.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: pikipixel@proton.me
